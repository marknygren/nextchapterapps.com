Reporting a bug

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Which app you’re using
  • Your device (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Pro)
  • iOS version
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • What happened vs. what you expected

Parents

If you’re a parent with questions about your child’s privacy or want to review your child’s data, email us at contact@nextchapterapps.com. We respond to privacy requests within 30 days.
