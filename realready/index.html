FAQ

Common questions

Does RealReady cover my state?

Yes — all 50 states, both national principles and state-specific rules.

Is it really free to start?

Yes. National-content categories and Quick 10 are free. State-specific categories are an optional upgrade.

What topics are covered?

Nine categories including Property & Land Use, Contracts, Agency, Finance, Real Estate Math, and state-specific rules.

Is this the same as my pre-license course?

No. RealReady is exam prep — for after (or alongside) your required course, to make sure you pass the state exam itself.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Questions are cached locally so you can study without signal.

Do you guarantee I’ll pass?

No one can — but targeted practice and honest feedback on what you’re missing is the closest thing.
