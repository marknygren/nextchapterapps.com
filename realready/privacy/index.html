Privacy Policy

Effective: May 1, 2026 · Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Next Chapter Education LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with the RealReady mobile application (the “App”). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

Next Chapter Education LLC is a limited liability company formed under the laws of the State of Montana, United States. We are the data controller responsible for your personal information under this policy.

  • Contact: contact@nextchapterapps.com
  • Role under EU / UK GDPR: Data Controller
  • Role under CCPA / CPRA: Business

For matters relating to data protection, use the email address above. We will respond within thirty (30) days.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your precise or approximate geolocation.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID) and do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect or store your payment card or bank account details — Apple processes all payments.
  • We do not sell or “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for the following purposes:

  1. Account management — creating and authenticating accounts, maintaining sessions.
  2. Service delivery — presenting study content tailored to your selected state, recording your quiz and reading progress.
  3. Purchase fulfillment — managing premium entitlement and restoration of previous purchases.
  4. Customer support — responding to feedback, feature requests, and account inquiries.
  5. Product improvement — analyzing aggregate, de-identified patterns to improve question accuracy, content quality, and user experience.
  6. Security and integrity — detecting abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  7. Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law.

6. International Data Transfers

Our sub-processors are located in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States — including the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland — your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

For transfers of personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we rely on:

  • EU–US Data Privacy Framework (and the UK and Swiss extensions), where the recipient is certified, or
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (2021/914 module 2, Controller-to-Processor) supplemented by Transfer Impact Assessments where required.

You may request a copy of the applicable transfer mechanism by emailing contact@nextchapterapps.com.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 Universal Rights

Regardless of where you live, you may:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete;
  • Delete your account and associated personal information (see Section 9);
  • Object to certain processing, including direct communications;
  • Receive a copy of your information in a portable, machine-readable format;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

8.2 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the additional rights to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose;
  • Delete personal information we have collected;
  • Correct inaccurate personal information;
  • Opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of personal information;
  • Limit the use of sensitive personal information;
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA, and we do not use sensitive personal information for purposes that would trigger a right to limit.

To submit a request, email contact@nextchapterapps.com from the address associated with your account. We will verify your identity before responding. You may designate an authorized agent in writing.

8.3 EU / UK Residents (GDPR)

In addition to the rights above, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection supervisory authority. A list is available at edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en.

8.4 Other U.S. States

We extend equivalent access, correction, deletion, and opt-out rights to residents of all U.S. states that grant them under applicable law (including Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, Oregon, Montana, Tennessee, Indiana, Iowa, Delaware, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Kentucky, Minnesota, Maryland, and Rhode Island).

8.5 Opt-Out Preference Signals

We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) and equivalent browser/device signals where required by applicable law. Because the App does not use targeted advertising or “sell” data, GPC signals have no current effect, but we will treat them as valid opt-out requests if our practices change.

9. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated personal data at any time:

  • In-app: Profile tab → Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is processed within 30 days.
  • By email: send a request to contact@nextchapterapps.com from the email address associated with your account.

Upon deletion we remove your profile, quiz attempts, reading history, gamification data, and feedback identifiers. Some records may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days before final erasure, and we may retain limited records where required for legal compliance (tax, fraud prevention, dispute resolution).

Guest-mode data is local to your device; uninstalling the App or clearing local storage deletes it immediately.

10. Children’s Privacy

RealReady is intended for users 18 years of age or older who are preparing for state real-estate licensing examinations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13, and our service is not directed to children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided information may contact contact@nextchapterapps.com.

11. Security

We employ industry-standard safeguards to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) for all network communication;
  • Encryption at rest for data stored by Supabase;
  • Secure device storage for authentication tokens (Apple Keychain via expo-secure-store on native devices);
  • Role-based access controls and Row-Level Security policies on our database;
  • Regular dependency updates and security patching.

No system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but will promptly address any incidents in accordance with applicable law.

12. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use your personal information for any automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you. Quiz scoring is deterministic (correct/incorrect) and does not constitute profiling under GDPR Art. 22.

13. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites, including state real-estate commission pages referenced in study articles. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy notices.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page;
  • Post a notice within the App;
  • For significant changes affecting existing users, provide at least 14 days’ advance notice where practicable.

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
